## Humans follow the same structured process as computers: input ‚Üí process ‚Üí output

- [Your friend]: "Where is your dog?"

From your friend's perspective, the question is an output‚Äîthey're sending information out. For you, it's an input. Your brain processes the question and quickly finds the answer, which becomes your output, sent back as a response.


It‚Äôs literally the same structure that computers use:
```
    Input ‚Äì You receive data (the question).

    Process ‚Äì You think about the data.

    Output ‚Äì You come up with and send the answer.
``` 
